# Release Notes - v3.2.0

## Compatibility

EOSFactory [v3.2.0](https://github.com/tokenika/eosfactory/releases/tag/v3.2.0) is compatible with EOS [v1.7.1](https://github.com/EOSIO/eos/releases/tag/v1.7.1) and EOSIO.CDT [v1.6.1](https://github.com/EOSIO/eosio.cdt/releases/tag/v1.6.1).

## New features in v3.2.0

* Native compilation and debugging of EOSIO smart contracts.
* Improved startup of the `nodeos` process: better control on stuck conditions that frequently occur.
